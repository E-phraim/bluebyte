Hello, I am Chinedu Ogosi

Management Consultant

Chinedu Ogosi is a seasoned banker, academician, and Lagos Business School alumnus with 25 plus years in banking, academia, and management consulting. Having a strong educational background, including a Senior Management Programme, Lagos Business School, Ph.D. in Management, University of Nigeria, M.Sc. in Management, University of Nigeria, B.Sc. in Geology, University of Nigeria.

Work experience

Chinedu has extensive experience in teaching corporate strategy and management science at a renowned Nigerian university, fostering student learning through innovative methodologies and practical insights. He specializes in operations research, building and leading high-performance teams to boost productivity and cut costs. He has served as Module Leader for Corporate Strategy at the University of Port Harcourt, Business School, Module Leader for Risk Management at Garden City Premier Business School, Port Harcourt and General Management at First City Monument Bank, Port Harcourt.

Professional skills

Chinedu has written essays and publications in major journals including International Journal of Current Research (2013) and European Journal of Scientific Research (2015).
